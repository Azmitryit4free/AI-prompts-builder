💡 Beginner tips
🎯
More detail = better results
Select 8–15 options. AI models thrive on specific, vivid descriptions rather than vague terms.
🚫
Negative prompts matter
Always fill in what to avoid — prevents blurry faces, watermarks, extra limbs, and bad anatomy.
💡
Lighting transforms everything
Try "volumetric god rays" or "chiaroscuro" — this single setting has the biggest visual impact.
🎨
Name your exact colors
"Teal and orange Hollywood grade" gives far more consistent results than just saying "colorful".
📷
Camera angle = storytelling
Low angles feel powerful. Dutch tilt adds tension. Bird's eye feels detached and observational.
